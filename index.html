Hey there, I'm Micahel, a PhD student at The NLP Research Lab at the CS faculty Technion. My research focuses on explainability in multi-modal models, where I aim to gain a better understanding of how information is encoded and how calculations are made in text-to-image models, large language models, and vision-language models.
